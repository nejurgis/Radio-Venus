music echoes the universe

Why does a certain melody, a bird's song, or the rhythmic hum of a mechanical apparatus sway into our deepest selves? Music removes the hard earth from underneath our feet, turns us to watery swamps, blows us away like feather clouds into the golden sunlight, invokes fire within us and burns us down, finally returning us back to earth, changed...

These cycles visit us at different times throughout our lives. In the form of a teenage love song. A rhythmic banger that held you on the dance floor all night. A playlist that a loved once made for you. A quiet moment by yourself on a bench, when you needed to take a minute.

Music is energy. It pushes and pulls, lifts up and throws down‚Äîthat is the only way to explain its effect on us.
